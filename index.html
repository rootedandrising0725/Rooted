The Miscarriage Nobody Talks About: My Story of Loss, Faith & Trying Again

November 2024 changed everything. Here's what I wish someone had told me.

I was 21 years old when I found out I was pregnant for the first time. I was terrified and excited and completely unprepared — and then, just as quickly as the joy came, it was gone. My miscarriage in November 2024 was the loneliest experience of my life. Not because people didn't care, but because nobody knew what to say. Nobody talked about it.

So I'm going to. Because you deserve to know that grief after pregnancy loss is real, it is heavy, and it does not mean your faith isn't strong enough. And I'm going to tell you what happened next — how one month later, I found out I was pregnant again, and how I carried that baby girl in both fear and fierce hope...
